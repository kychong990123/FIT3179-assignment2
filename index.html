Indian Food Statistics

Both South and West regions of India have 3 dishes that are non vegetarian .
Both East and North regions of India also have the same number of non vegetarian dishes which is 5.
Central does not have any non vegetarian dishes at all.





Sour and bitter dishes are not popular in India, there are only a total of 4 sour dishes and 1 bitter dish in the whole country according to the given dataset.
Punjab has the most spicy dishes.
West Bengal has the most sweet dishes.
Gujarat is the state with a sour dish.
Gujarat and Punjab are the only states with bitter dishes. Each has two bitter dishes respectively.
